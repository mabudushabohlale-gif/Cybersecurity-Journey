# Cybersecurity-Progress-Journey

## 👤 About Me
I am a 15-year-old aspiring Information Technology (IT) student. I created this repository to document my technical growth, lab notes, and the concepts I master as I work towards a career in cybersecurity.

## 🎯 My Goals
Foundation: Master the fundamentals of networking, Linux, and the OSI model.

Certification: Study for and eventually achieve entry-level certifications like CompTIA Security+, CompTIA Network+, eJPT, and SAL1.

Practical Skills: Move from theory to hands-on practice by completing labs on platforms like TryHackMe.

Specialization: Explore both defensive and offensive security to decide on a career path in penetration testing or as a security analyst.

## 📈 Why I Am Tracking My Progress on GitHub
Accountability: Publicly documenting my journey keeps me motivated and consistent.

Proof of Work: To build a living portfolio that demonstrates my technical writing skills and hands-on experience to future mentors and employers.

Knowledge Base: To create a searchable personal library of commands, configurations, and walkthroughs that I can refer back to at any time.
